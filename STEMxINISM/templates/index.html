Who we are

We are women of science and technology . We are volunteers, with a mission. A computer scientist, a biologist and a few other women who want to change the status quo of women who are not treated the same as men in STEM, and hence lose out on opportunities . Join us in helping, inspiring, supporting and motivating women and help break the glass ceilings of inherent patriarchy

What we do

Many women eventually drop out of STEM fields because of organisational barriers to career progression, lack of career guidance and support, and family commitments. The same is not true for men who work in STEM. Although many women successfully balance their careers and family responsibilities, there are still institutional obstacles for women in STEM. We want to break the gender gap, identify and raise the profile of women in STEM by encouraging them to learn and grow by giving them role models and mentors who share their knowledge and experiences in their respective fields.
